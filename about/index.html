about

My name is Zhao Lü. Zhao is pronounced as 'ciao' with a 'j' and it means to encourage oneself in Chinese. I was born in Beijing, China. I've lived in Arizona, California, London, New York, and São Paulo. I'm permanently based in San Francisco and I travel quite a bit.

I'm a programmer who enjoys practicing yoga.

I have worked in R&D for big names like IBM, Orange, and O2, and helped smaller start-ups develop their technical chops. As a developer, I am interested in different programming languages (Ruby, Java, Erlang, C). I like to tinker with hardware such as Arduino and Beagleboard. In 2012, I attended Deborah Burkman's yoga retreat in Koh Samui, Thailand. After the retreat, I started my passion project, ForYogi, to promote yoga as a life style.

I now spend time between ForYogi, consulting, and advising start-ups.

In my spare time, I like to read, take photos with my rangefinder camera, biking and hiking. I used to spend a lot of time running (road and trail) until knee injuries stopped me. I spent a good amount of time playing indoor and sand volleyball as well as basketball. I used to surf in Santa Cruz and Hawaii.
